# Noteship — Product Vision & Positioning

## Purpose

Define what Noteship is, who it is for, and what success looks like for the MVP.

## One-liner

**Noteship helps solo consultants and coaches instantly find and reuse their own knowledge by turning notes into publishable content.**

## Problem

Solo professionals create lots of knowledge (notes, meeting takeaways, frameworks), but later:

- They **can’t find** what they wrote because they don’t remember the exact wording.
- They **waste time rewriting** the same ideas.
- Publishing to LinkedIn is **high effort** and inconsistent.

## Target customer

Primary: **Solo consultants / coaches** (one-person businesses) who:

- run client calls and workshops
- create frameworks and repeatable content
- publish to build authority and inbound leads

## Core value proposition

1. **Semantic memory**: find earlier ideas without exact keywords.
2. **Repurposing**: turn notes into polished posts quickly.
3. **Publish & schedule**: ship content to LinkedIn reliably.

## Differentiation

- Semantic-first retrieval (not just keyword search)
- AI workflows tied to a personal note base
- Simple “note → post → publish” pipeline

## MVP boundaries

In scope:

- Notes (TipTap editor), stored as Markdown in S3
- Semantic search across notes
- AI draft generation from a note
- Integrations: LinkedIn (publish now + schedule)
- Plans + entitlements (feature gating; quotas)

Out of scope (MVP):

- Analytics (post performance dashboards)
- Teams / workspaces
- Advanced collaboration, comments
- Deep in-note semantic highlighting (future)

## Success metrics (MVP)

- Activation: user creates ≥3 notes and runs ≥3 searches in week 1
- Retention: weekly active users returning to search notes
- Conversion: upgrade to paid plan for scheduling / higher limits
- Reliability: publish success rate and low failure retries

## Non-goals (explicit)

- Competing with Notion as a general-purpose workspace
- Building a social media analytics suite in MVP
- Building a generic “Zapier-like” integration platform immediately
