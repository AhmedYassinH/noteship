# Noteship — Messaging & Positioning Guide

_Last updated: 2026-01-28_

## 1) Positioning (simple)

**Noteship is an AI-first notes workflow for knowledge workers who publish.**  
It helps you **recall ideas by meaning**, **repurpose notes into grounded drafts**, and **publish consistently** (LinkedIn + Medium).

## 2) Primary message pillars (the 3-step promise)

1. **Recall (semantic-powered)**
   - Find and resurface ideas by meaning, not keywords.
2. **Repurpose (grounded drafts)**
   - Turn notes into structured drafts in your voice.
3. **Publish & schedule (reliable shipping)**
   - Ship to LinkedIn/Medium with scheduling and clear status.

## 3) Differentiation (what makes switching worth it)

- Not generic note storage: **recall + resurfacing**, built for knowledge work
- Not generic AI writing: **drafts grounded in your own notes**, not internet fluff
- Not just scheduling: **a single workflow** from note → draft → publish

## 4) Words to use / avoid

### Use

- recall, meaning-based, resurfacing, grounded drafts, repurpose, your voice, consistency, calm workflow, publish reliably, schedule, export

### Avoid

- magic, viral, hack, growth cheat, “AI that writes everything”, robot metaphors

## 7) Landing page headline sets (examples)

### Set A (pipeline-first)

- H1: **Turn notes into publish-ready drafts.**
- Sub: Recall your thinking by meaning, repurpose it in your voice, and publish consistently — without rewriting from scratch.

### Set B (consultant-specific)

- H1: **Stop rewriting what you already know.**
- Sub: Recall past insights, convert them to structured drafts, and publish to LinkedIn/Medium on schedule.

### Set C (authority-building)

- H1: **Build authority from your own thinking.**
- Sub: Noteship helps you recall, repurpose, and publish — with consistent, reliable workflows.

### Set D (recall-forward, but not “search tool”)

- H1: **Recall ideas by meaning — then ship them.**
- Sub: Find what you meant, turn it into a draft, and publish reliably.
