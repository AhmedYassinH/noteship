# Noteship — Messaging & Positioning Guide

_Last updated: 2026-01-24_

## 1) Positioning (simple)

**Noteship is semantic memory for knowledge workers who publish.**  
It helps you **find ideas by meaning**, **reuse them**, and **publish consistently** (LinkedIn + Medium).

## 2) Primary message pillars

1. **Semantic recall**
   - Search by meaning, not keywords.
2. **Reuse & repurpose**
   - Turn your notes into structured drafts quickly.
3. **Publish & schedule**
   - Ship to LinkedIn/Medium reliably, with scheduling.

## 3) Differentiation (what makes switching worth it)

- Not generic note storage: **meaning-based retrieval**
- Not generic AI writing: **grounded in your own knowledge**
- Not just scheduling: **note → draft → publish pipeline**

## 4) Words to use / avoid

### Use

- semantic memory, recall, reuse, clarity, your voice, consistency, grounded drafts, publish reliably, calm workflow

### Avoid

- magic, viral, hack, growth cheat, “AI that writes everything”, robot metaphors

- **Remember by meaning. Publish with consistency.**
- **Your ideas, findable. Your writing, shippable.**
- **Search your thinking. Ship your content.**
- **From notes to posts — grounded in you.**
