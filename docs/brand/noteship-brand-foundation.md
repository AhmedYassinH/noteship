# Noteship — Brand Foundation

_Last updated: 2026-01-24_

## 1) Brand essence

**Noteship is a calm, professional “second brain” that helps solo consultants and coaches recall their own ideas by meaning and turn them into publishable content.**

### One-line promise

**Remember by meaning. Reuse with confidence. Publish with consistency.**

## 2) Brand archetype (locked)

- **Primary:** **Sage** — clarity, insight, mastery, trustworthy guidance
- **Secondary:** **Creator** — make, craft, ship output consistently

**Resulting personality:** quiet authority + practical creation.

## 3) Who it is for (MVP)

Primary ICP:

- Solo consultants / coaches
- Knowledge workers building authority via writing

## 4) The problem we solve (in human terms)

- “I remember the idea, not the wording.”
- “I keep rewriting the same thinking.”
- “Publishing consistently is hard when I’m busy.”

## 5) What Noteship is (and is not)

### It **is**

- Semantic-first note recall
- Note → draft → publish/schedule workflow
- Portability-minded (Markdown)

### It is **not**

- A general-purpose workspace competing with Notion
- A generic “AI writer” that invents content from nothing
- A social analytics suite (MVP explicitly avoids analytics)

## 6) Brand values (behavioral)

- **Clarity over hype**
- **Trust over gimmicks**
- **Craft over noise**
- **Consistency over novelty**
- **User control over “magic”**

## 7) Tone rules

Do:

- Calm, concise, confident
- Precise language and concrete outcomes
- Treat the user as competent

Avoid:

- “AI magic”, “hack”, “instant fame”
- Internet-slang, meme tone
- Overpromising (“guaranteed leads”, “viral”)

## 8) Visual feel (high-level)

- Minimal, editorial, readable
- “Paper + precision” rather than “neon SaaS”
- Subtle depth, restrained accents

## 9) North-star user feeling

When opening Noteship late at night after client calls:

> “Everything I learned is here. I can find it fast. I can ship something good.”
