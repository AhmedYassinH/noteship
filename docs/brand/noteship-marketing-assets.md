# Noteship — Marketing Assets Checklist (MVP)

_Last updated: 2026-01-28_

## 1) Required pages (public)

- Landing page (EN/AR)
- Pricing
- Login / Signup
- Terms, Privacy, Cookies
- Security / Data handling (simple trust page)
- Changelog (optional but good)

## 2) Landing page sections (recommended order)

1. Hero: value proposition + primary CTA
2. Problem: “I remember the idea, not the wording”
3. How it works: **Note → Recall → Repurpose → Publish/Schedule**
4. Key features (3 pillars)
5. Screenshots (editor, draft builder, publish status)
   - Optional/secondary: recall/search results view
6. Pricing (free vs pro)
7. FAQ (integrations, privacy, billing)
8. Final CTA

## 3) Core visuals to produce

- Product screenshots (light and dark)
- One “workflow” diagram (simple)
- Optional: short looping demo video (10–20s)

## 4) Copy assets

- 3–5 headline variants (EN/AR)
- 10 microcopy snippets (buttons, empty states)
- 5 FAQs (EN/AR)
- 2 short founder-style posts (dogfooding on LinkedIn)

## 5) Social launch assets

- Product Hunt style thumbnails (if used)
- LinkedIn post templates (EN/AR)
- Simple “coming soon” banner

## 6) Consistency rules

- Keep editorial tone (no hype)
- Keep visuals minimal
- Use the same pillar verbs everywhere: **Recall / Repurpose / Publish**
